The Best Movies According to Noura

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Invisible Guest

The plot twist of the movie is amazing. I was shocked at the end. It will always be one of my best movies and I always recommend it to anyone I meet.

Knives Out

This movie is hilarious, one of the funniest comedy movies ever. Totally recommended.

The Greatest Showman

The songs in the movie are incredible; you will never get tired of hearing them. Best musical movie ever.
